The Opportunity

Why projects bleed — and how to stop it

The Problem

Construction projects don't fail all at once — they bleed through cascading delays. A late subcontractor blocks the next trade. A material delivery that was "fine" becomes a crisis. Milestones slip quietly until the cost overrun is undeniable.

  • ✕ Schedules managed across fragmented spreadsheets and text threads
  • ✕ No early warning when a delay is about to cascade downstream
  • ✕ Budget overruns surface too late to course-correct

The Solution

A unified operational command centre where milestones, subcontractor schedules, and material deliveries are tracked together with dependency awareness — surfacing risk before it cascades so managers act early, not late.

  • ✓ Real-time visibility across all active projects in one view
  • ✓ Dependency-aware milestone and subcontractor tracking
  • ✓ At-risk alerts that flag cascades before they start
