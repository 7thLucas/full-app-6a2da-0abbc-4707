Weekly Verified Operations

Projected impact — based on your inputs. Estimates from stated assumptions, not measured results.

What counts as a verified operation?

Milestone status logged — PM marks a milestone complete, at-risk, or delayed
Delivery confirmed — material delivery logged as received, rescheduled, or missed
Sub check-in recorded — subcontractor schedule update logged (arrived / delayed / complete)

Not counted: logins, page views, or passive reads

= 45 WVO / week
