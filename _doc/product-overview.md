# Product Overview

## Product
**Working Title**: Construction Project Manager App *(name TBD — to be confirmed with user)*
**Category**: Operational / Project Management
**Platform**: Web app

---

## Users
**Primary persona**: Construction project managers responsible for coordinating field operations, trade subcontractors, and material supply chains across active job sites.

They operate under schedule pressure, manage multiple projects simultaneously, and are accountable for on-time delivery and budget integrity — often without unified tooling to surface the interdependencies between milestones, trades, and material flow.

---

## The Problem
Construction projects don't fail all at once — they bleed through cascading delays. A subcontractor arrives three days late; that blocks the next trade; the material delivery that was "fine" is now a crisis. Milestones slip quietly until the cost overrun is undeniable. Project managers currently manage this across fragmented spreadsheets, text threads, and memory — with no early warning system when a delay is about to cascade downstream, and no single view of budget vs. actuals in real time.

---

## The Solution
A unified operational command center for construction project managers to track milestones, subcontractor schedules, and material deliveries — all in one place — so that delay risk is surfaced early, accountability is clear, and projects finish on time and within budget.

---

## Core Features (MVP Scope)

1. **Milestone Tracker** — create, assign, and monitor project milestones with dependency awareness; flag at-risk items before they cascade
2. **Subcontractor Schedule Board** — manage trade schedules with planned vs. actual arrival and work-completion status; no more chasing via text
3. **Material Delivery Tracker** — log expected and actual delivery dates; link deliveries to dependent milestone tasks so gaps surface immediately
4. **Budget vs. Actual Monitor** — track spend against budget at project and phase level with variance alerts before overruns become irreversible
5. **At-Risk Alerts** — early warning system that flags delays likely to cascade to downstream tasks, trades, or deliveries
6. **Project Dashboard** — single-view health status across all active projects: schedule, budget, delivery, and subcontractor status at a glance

---

## Positioning
A practical, no-nonsense operations tool purpose-built for the construction industry — not a generic project management SaaS awkwardly adapted for construction. Built around the real workflow of a job site: milestones depend on trades, trades depend on materials, materials depend on schedule. The app reflects those dependencies.

---

## Brand Tone
Professional, direct, and reliable. The language of the job site, not the boardroom. Functional over decorative. Trust through clarity and precision.

---

## Strategic Principles
- **Every delay caught early is money saved** — the app exists to surface delay risk before it cascades, not to document it afterward
- **Single source of truth** — milestones, subcontractor schedules, and material deliveries live in one view, not three spreadsheets
- **Accountability without bureaucracy** — quick to update, easy to read, built for people who are on-site and on the move

---

## Scope Notes
MVP covers: milestone tracking with dependency awareness, subcontractor schedule management, material delivery tracking, basic budget tracking, and a unified project health dashboard.

Post-MVP candidates: photo documentation per milestone, daily logs, RFI/submittal tracking, client-facing progress reports.
